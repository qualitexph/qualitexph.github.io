Connect with Us!

QualitEx was originated on December 1999 as answer to the challenge of globalization among various industries specifically the manufacturing and servicing sector.

The Logo depicts a 'Company showing a Quantum Leap in Performance through time when implementing a System in their business process'. The company (client) needs an 'Enabler' to work on their System in order to realize this performance which in the end will result in satisfying their Customer by providing a quality product, timely delivery and become a cost effective operating Company.

"In the past, business was considered secure if it is competitive in any of these aspects: cost and productivity, quality and service, or speed and flexibility. Today, to maintain a good business, it is essential to excel in all three at the same time. Companies must provide high quality products, quickly alacritous service with a relatively low cost."



Edison Pike
President / Managing Consultant

Mission

To help the small and medium scale industries that are experiencing flat or decreasing growth in terms of capitalization and /or profitability in the design, development and implementation of Quality Systems, tailored to fit their financial capability enabling them to compete in GLOBALIZATION by:

  • Bridging the gap between theory and application
  • Integrating human and technological resources in order to balance individual and total system needs.

Vision

QualitEx is a leader, customer-focused consulting company, well-admired by its clients, and a benchmark of 'speed, flexibility and quality service' among all the business and management consulting firms in the Philippines.

Objectives

To provide a consultancy and training services in the specialized technology of Quality Management. It covers product inspection and reliability; setting up a Quality/Environmental system in compliance with ISO Standard; installation of Continuous Improvement Program and Business Process Reengineering.
